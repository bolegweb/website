28-29 September 2016: INSPIRE Conference 2016, Barcelona, Spain.

Dr. Kliment presented two contributions at the INSPIRE conference. Bolegweb project was presented in the poster session and related mobileWFS project in the parallel oral session.

7 September 2016: First International Conference on Smart Data and Smart Cities, Split, Croatia.

Dr. Kliment presented contribution entitled Using Participatory Approach to Improve Availability of Spatial Data for Local Government reporting the results from the research study realized in 2015 with collegues Dr. Vlado Cetl, Dr. Hrvoje Tomic, Msc. Josip Lisjak and Dr. Marcel Kliment last year in the city of Pozega.

Presentation
9 June 2016: 4th Workshop of the DEMLAS project, Stari Grad, Island of Hvar, Croatia.

Dr. Kliment presented together with other colleagues from the faculty of geodesy his contribution to the DEMLAS (Development of Multipurpose Land Administration System) project's data and metadata management system as a part of Bolegweb project knowledge transfer activity. DEMLAS project is currently implementing the metadata publication workflow based on OGC services and harvesting CSW-T operation, which has been designed and facilitated by Bolegweb project.

Presentation
22 April 2016: Lecture for Members of Croatian Geodetic Society, Split - Dalmatian County Association, Split, Croatia.

Dr. Kliment together with Prof. Cetl gave lectures to a mixed group of surveyors representing business, public and education sector from the Split and Dalmatia regions in Republic of Croatia. Four hours of lectures covered INSPIRE and National SDI in Croatia (Cetl) and Geospatial Web Services with practical examples of their usage (Kliment).

Dr. Kliment Participated to the Danube Baltic Open (Geo) Data Hackathon 2016, which was held 16–18 March 2016, in Riga Latvia. The goal of the event was to develop from scratch ideas and implement them. Together with Dr. M. Tuchyna and Ing. J.
